Renewable Energy Potential for India

India is the world's 3rd largest renewable energy producer

As of October 2024, India's renewable capacity is over 200 GW!

This massive growth helps combat pollution and secures a sustainable future for Chennai and all of India.

Renewable Energy Solutions

Solar Energy

Harnesses the sun's power through photovoltaic cells, producing clean electricity with zero emissions.

Did you know? The amount of solar energy that reaches Earth in one hour could meet global energy needs for an entire year!

Wind Energy

Converts kinetic energy from wind into electricity using turbines, providing sustainable power for communities.

Wind energy can reduce carbon emissions by up to 99% compared to fossil fuels.

Tips for Sustainability

  • 01

    Reduce Single-Use Plastics

    Switch to reusable water bottles, shopping bags, and food containers to minimize plastic waste.

  • 02

    Support Renewable Energy

    Consider switching to a green energy provider or installing solar panels if possible.

  • 03

    Conserve Energy

    Turn off lights and unplug devices when not in use to reduce your carbon footprint.

  • 04

    Choose Sustainable Transportation

    Walk, bike, carpool, or use public transportation whenever possible.
